- THE PROJECT -

Skate wall is a project born in LOLA Madrid in collaboration with Nomad Skateboards to decorate the walls of the agency in Barcelona. They gave us 10 recycle boards to create a new artistic expressions and this is our proposal.

- WHY -

Our skateboard contains a message because we believe that every artistic expression, however small, try (or at least should try) to convey something; generate a reaction in the person facing it.

"Be brave little fellow" is the idea we want to convey each one of us who spend our days in the agency; be brave and believe that you are capable to do anything you set your mind to. We are convinced tht things that scare you really are worth trying in all aspects of life. "If it scares you, might be a good thing to try".

And to attract attention, we decided to add to the skateboard an interactive element: When you're passing in front of it, you hear sounds of trees, and if you get near the board, the eyes of a hidden animal lights on amid a roar sound.

- HOW -

The basis of the work is a digital illustration printed on vinyl. It is hand painted, but throught Photoshop brushes that simulate a real paintbrush, respecting different variables such as hand pressure and the type of brush tip.
From this, we leverage technology to give more power to the idea and the interaction that generates leads the message to have more impact. Behind the table, we have installed a small circuit based on Arduino that controls a proximity sensor, red LEDs and a small speaker.

Thanks to the proximity sensor we know if we have someone in front of the table, how far and for how long. Based on these inputs we can decide waht sounds, stored in a mini sound card, can shoot. These sounds are amplified with another small chip that directs them to a loudspeaker. At the same time, we make the red LEDs embedded in the wood blink.
